Bibliography

References and sources for the collection

Primary Sources

The information in this collection is drawn from a variety of primary and secondary sources, including:

Frank Lloyd Wright

  • Wright, Frank Lloyd. An Autobiography. Duell, Sloan and Pearce, 1943.
  • Wright, Frank Lloyd. The Natural House. Horizon Press, 1954.
  • Wright, Frank Lloyd. In the Cause of Architecture. Architectural Record, 1908-1952.

Mid-Century Modern Design

  • Hiesinger, Kathryn B. and George H. Marcus. Design Since 1945. Philadelphia Museum of Art, 1983.
  • Pulos, Arthur J. American Design Ethic: A History of Industrial Design to 1940. MIT Press, 1983.
  • Sparke, Penny. Design in Italy, 1870 to the Present. Abbeville Press, 1988.

Educational Philosophy

  • Froebel, Friedrich. The Education of Man. A. Lovell & Company, 1887.
  • Brosterman, Norman. Inventing Kindergarten. Harry N. Abrams, 1997.
  • Liebscher, Peter. Art and Architecture in the Service of Politics. MIT Press, 1993.

Secondary Sources

Additional research materials include academic journals, museum catalogs, and specialized publications on design history and architectural theory.

Archival Materials

This collection incorporates information from various archival sources, including museum collections, private archives, and historical documentation related to the artifacts and their creators.
